Your Personality Color

Blue

Lovers of blue have a deep need to find inner peace and truth, to live their life according to their ideals and beliefs without having to change their inflexible viewpoint of life to satisfy others.

Orange

If orange is your favorite color, you have a great need to be with people, to socialize with them, and be accepted and respected as part of a group. You also have a need for challenges in your life, whether it is physical or social challenges.

Red

Red as your favorite color. Red can have aggressive tendency.Often exhibiting a violent temper - this is negative passion and energy. You flare up instantaneously but calm down quite quickly once you get it out of your system and then forget it ever happened - you do not hold a grudge.

White

white is your personality color, your deepest need is for simplicity in your own life and to be independent and self-reliant so you do not need to depend on anyone else.

Green

If you love Green you have a deep need to belong, to love and be loved, and to feel safe and secure. You have love for nature and the environment.

Purple

If purple is your color, you are a non-conformist who sees life from a different point of view.

Yellow

If your color is yellow, you are bright energetic personality. You communicate well on a mental level with like-minded people, but can become bitter and sharp-tongued if crossed.
